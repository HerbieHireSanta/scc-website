The Vetting Process

Anyone can put on a red suit. What separates our Santas isn't the costume ‚Äî it's everything that happens before they ever arrive at your event. Here's how we screen every entertainer in our network:

01

Application Review

Every prospective Santa submits a detailed application. We review experience, event history, and references before proceeding.

02

Background Check

No exceptions. Every Santa is run through a comprehensive background screening. This has been our policy since before it was common practice.

03

In-Person Interview

We meet every Santa personally. We assess presence, warmth, professionalism, and how they handle unexpected situations.

04

Performance Standards

We review appearance (real beards preferred), costume quality, and verify the entertainer can maintain character for the full duration of an event.

05

Ongoing Evaluation

We collect client feedback after every event. Santas who don't meet our standards aren't re-booked. Simple as that.

06

Professionalism Standards

Timeliness, communication, and conduct are non-negotiable. Our Santas represent our 50+ year reputation every time they show up.
